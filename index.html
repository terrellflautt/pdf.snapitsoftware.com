Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF tool completely free?

Yes! Process up to 3 PDF files daily (25MB each) completely free with no registration required. For unlimited access, upgrade to our Professional plan at $9/month.

How do I split a PDF into multiple files?

Upload your PDF, choose "Split" mode, specify pages or ranges (like "1,3,5-10"), and download individual files. Perfect for extracting specific chapters or sections.

Can I merge PDFs in a specific order?

Absolutely! Upload multiple PDFs and drag them to reorder before merging. This ensures your combined document has pages in the exact sequence you want.

Is my data secure when processing PDFs?

Yes, all processing happens securely. Files are processed either locally in your browser or on our encrypted servers, then automatically deleted within 24 hours.

What's the maximum file size for free users?

Free users can process files up to 25MB each, 3 files per day. Professional users get 100MB files with 100 daily processing limit.

How much can I compress a PDF file?

Compression varies by content, but typically reduces file size by 30-90%. Image-heavy PDFs compress more than text-only documents while maintaining visual quality.

Can I convert PDF to Word for editing?

Yes! Convert PDFs to Word (.docx), Excel (.xlsx), or PowerPoint (.pptx) formats. Text, images, and basic formatting are preserved for easy editing.

How does this compare to Adobe Acrobat?

Our tools offer the most common PDF operations for free, while Adobe Acrobat requires expensive subscriptions. Perfect for occasional use and basic PDF processing needs.
